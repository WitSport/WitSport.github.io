About Modern Business

Our company’s goal is to provide college students, focusing on Wentworth students to get out of their dorm room and provide information about all of the great things happening on campus and around Boston!

We started this business, because we realized that, even though all students at Wentworth get a weekly email about events going on, that isn’t all that is going on, we want to provide a place that has a plethora of information with things that college students might be interested in, other than the few highlights that we get directly. We miss so much because of a lack of communication, so we have made it our goal to get that information out there!
